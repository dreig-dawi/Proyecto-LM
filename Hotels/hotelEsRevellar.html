Es Revellar Art Resort


Location: Camí des Revellar Km 3.7 Pol 4 Parc 74, 07630 Campos, Illes Balears, España.

Capacity: 8 adults.

Category: Villa.

Information: Situated a few kilometres from the best Mediterranean beaches, the four stars complex Es Revellar Art Resort will give you the best services and installations to benefit from the best accommodation during your holidays. In your visit, you will enjoy largest gardens which collect one of the largest art collections in Mallorca. Our resort is the perfect place to celebrate any type of event: weddings, banquets, communions, private meetings... considering that its landscaped zone is equipped with a terrace and a tent characterize for its spectacularity and elegance that make the space the perfect scenary.

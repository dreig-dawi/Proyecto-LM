Information: Son Cosmet is an old Mallorcan “possessió” of 150 hectares converted into an exclusive hotel in which tradition and innovation go hand in hand harmoniously. The construction, begun in the 13th century, with a defense tower from the 16th century, is unique in the area due to its ancestral characteristics. Large living rooms, wine cellar, terraces, porches, gardens, a spectacular pool, fields and forests constitute a dream setting. The furniture is made up of valuable pieces from the 17th and 18th centuries originating from the house and others from family inheritance. A careful and discreet service ensures that the guests' slightest wishes are fulfilled.

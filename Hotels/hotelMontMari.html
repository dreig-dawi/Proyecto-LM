MontMari Apartment

Location: Jaume Ferrer, 15, Palma.

Capacity: 2 viajeros.

Category: Turismo de interior.

Information: Located in Palma de Mallorca, Montmari, Turismo de Interior are set in the historic centre in Plaça Drassana. They offer air-conditioned accommodation with free Wi-Fi, and a shared sun terrace. Montmari, Turismo de Interior feature stylish décor. All include a lounge area with satellite TV, comfortable beds, sheets and towels and a kitchen with dishwasher, microwave and hob. The Montmari, Turismo de Interior are set just 100 meters from the port and 2 minutes’ walk from the 15th century Sa Llotja. Palma Cathedral and the Almudaina Palace are 5 minutes’ walk away. Montmari, Turismo de Interior is 10 minutes’ walk from Plaça Major and the Arabic baths. It is located 10 km from Palma Airport.

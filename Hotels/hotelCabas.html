Information: Finca Es Cabàs is the ideal place to feel the essence of rural Mallorca, being able to live first-hand very exclusive experiences that allow you to see life in the Mallorcan countryside today. It is located very close to Palma, just 15 minutes away, so it is very easily accessible. The origins of the estate date back to the Arab occupation in Mallorca, the restoration of the estate has been carried out preserving as much as possible the ancient essence of the construction. The lime kilns, the charcoal burners' houses, the sitjas and the old tafona have been restored to provide the visitor with a sample of the old country life on a farm in the foothills of the Tramuntana Mountains.
